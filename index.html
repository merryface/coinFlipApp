My Coin Flip

Click the button to flip the coin!

Ever need to flip a coin, but you can't because keeping cash on your person is a thing of the past? Follow these easy steps:

  1. Place cursor over "Flip!" button
  2. click your mouse
  3. if it says,"It's heads!" then it's heads, otherwise, it will say "It's tails"
